Our Journey

The Flintstones were founded under Ozel Cakabey Okullari for the 2025-2026 season (FLL Unearthed). We placed 3rd at the Aegean Regional Tournament, earned a spot at Nationals, and then became Turkey National Champions - beating 74 teams from 21 cities across Turkey.

Now we represent Turkey at the FIRST World Festival in Houston, USA - the world's largest science and technology tournament for youth, bringing together over 45 countries and 110,000+ team members.

Our coach Oguzhan Kose has guided teams to the Asia-Pacific World Championship over 16 years. Our 10-member team of 9th and 10th graders is almost entirely scholarship-funded - your sponsorship directly enables us to compete on the world stage.
