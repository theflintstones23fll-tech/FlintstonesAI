FlintAI - Innovation Project

Our Solution to Archaeological Fragmentation

The Problem

Fragments Lost in Time

Archaeologists excavating ancient sites routinely recover thousands of pottery shards, stone fragments, and broken artifacts - but lack effective systematic methods to match and reassemble them.

  • Thousands of fragments per excavation site
  • Manual matching takes months or years
  • Institutional knowledge lost when experts retire
  • Storage degradation damages fragments further
Our Solution

AI-Powered Fragment Matching

FlintAI is a web platform that uses computer vision and machine learning to analyze photographs of artifact fragments, classify them by structural characteristics, and suggest fragments with high matching potential.

Photo Analysis - Upload fragment photos; AI extracts shape, texture, and edge data
Smart Classification - Classify fragments by period, material, and form
Match Suggestions - Similarity scoring ranks candidate matches from database
Digital Archive - Preserve fragments digitally for future reconstruction
